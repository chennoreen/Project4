Most people share the common love and hate of online shopping. Good things about online shopping such as, Unlimited choice of goodies just a click away eliminates the need to go out of your cozy home and allows to shop more comfortable. Also, if you have to wait at least couple of weeks for delivery, a good chance is, that you've already forgotten about your order, so it's just like getting a present from yourself.

All of this sounds great, However, sometimes, online shopping can also leads to a disaster. There are millions of proofs that this perfect story can end up as an online shopping fail, that the products people received are way far from the initial expectation. Buying cosmetic products online is especially risky. Since people are all a wide range of colors and ethnically diverse, the products that fits well on others may not work perfectly on yourself.

As online customers can't physically access to the cosmetic products, all the product information they get is through digital format. Such as online customer reviews, recommandation from socail influencers, etc. Therefore, receiving products that are different from expectation is really common. While in the real world we don't really have this problem, we can phyisically see the prducts, touch it and test it. Therefore, this "online shopping fail lipstick" is a recreation of that in the real world form.
