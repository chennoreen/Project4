Welcome

A nice login system is necessary for every online shop. So I create one for Tryon Beauty. Since login is always the first thing to do when accessing to an online store, the login button is placed at the right hand corner with light yellow colors to distinguish from other elements, that make users easy to recognized. Different from the normal login page by other online shop, this login page have nice animation that may add a little fun during the process. As the users move the mouse onto the "username" box, it will turn from transparent to white to indicate the clickability. After finishing typing both username and password, the users can click on "login", and the text of "welcome" will show up right away with littel square boxs popping up filling up the pages.
