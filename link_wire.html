Tryon Servic Idea

This is a fun interaction illustrated the Tryon service. In the Tryon Beauty website, lipstick can be applied digitally to the users's lip by clikcking on the chosen colors, to see if the prducts match well on themselves. Therefore, this interaction was created to show this idea, that by clicking on the colors, users can be able to decide and change the colors of the model's lip and the background. The first button on the left hand side is for changing the lip colors. The following two buttons control the background colors. One is for the left and right sides of backgound, and the other is for the middle part of the background. Finally, the last button can randomly change the colors of the lip and background. overall, this interaction can engage the audiance to have a little fun and clearly indicate my idea of applying colors by clicking.
