Lipstick

 

The add to cart animation was created to add fun into the interaction, also let the users clearly awear that their chosen products have sucessfrully add to the shopping cart. By clicking on the "add to chart" button, the product will become smaller and faded. And after the product page fly into the chart, the chart will shake, so that the user will clearly know that their products are inside the chart.
