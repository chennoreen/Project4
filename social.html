Social media plays an important role for brands to establish their characteristic, and engage their customers to build up close relationship. Therefore, cosidering the importance of social media, I design the layout of social links for Tryon Beauty. As shown, all the links of different media connect with each other through a dotted line, and the icon of social media within the white circle will squeeze and bounce up as the users move the mouse onto them, which add a little fun into the interaction. By clicking on the icon, it will drive users to the social media page (While there is no physical social media page within the links)
